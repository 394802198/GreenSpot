  • How you choose your authentication method


1. Enable Moble Wechat and Membership authentication method

Under the Authentication tab you will find 3 authentication icons, simply by clicking each one of them to enable your Mobile Wechat and Membership Authentication method. and dont forget to click “Submit” afterwards.




2. Enable twitter/Facebook authentication method

To enable twitter/Facebook authentication you need to copy and paste your Facebook page or “twitter page” and “screen name” to your control panel, and dont forget to submit after setting up your authentication
